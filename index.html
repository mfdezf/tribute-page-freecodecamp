Ayrton Senna

Ayrton Senna 3 Times F1 World champion

This is a summary of Ayrton's life:

  • 1961 - Born in Sao Paulo Brazil.
  • 1965 - Takes interest in karts.
  • 1974 - First race in karting.
  • 1977 - Wins south american Kart Championship.
  • 1978 - Race in the Worl Kart Championship until 1980.
  • 1981 - Race in the Formula Ford.
  • 1982 - Race in European Formula Ford 2000 and won the Championship.
  • 1983 - Race in Formula Three and won the Championship.
  • 1984 - Started his carrer in Formula 1 with Toleman.
  • 1985 - Moves to Lotus Team until 1987.
  • 1988 - Moves to Mclaren and win his first title.
  • 1989 - Started the biggest rivalry in the F1 history with teammate Alain Prost.
  • 1990 - Won Second Championship.
  • 1991 - Won Third Championship.
  • 1994 - Moves to Williams seeking a most competitive car.
  • 1994 - Dies at age 34, leading the San Marino Grand Prix, in a tragic accident.
